Case Competition

Junior IT Case Competition (JICC)

  • In collaboration with WITM & Sponsored by Nando's
  • October 22, 2017 — 9 AM - 3 PM
  • Cara Commons — TRS 1-148
  • Business Formal

ITMSA and WITM are excited to announce that the annual Junior IT Case Competition (JICC) is back! This year, JICC will be held on October 22nd. Please note, the competition is exclusive to 1st and 2nd year BTM students.

Participating in this case competition will provide you with a chance to collaborate, build upon skills within a competitive learning environment, and leverage in-class material to solve business problems to a diverse set of industry professionals, all for a chance to win cash prizes! The team that comes in 1st place will get $1000, 2nd place will get $750 and the team in third place will get $450. Let's be real we are all students who could use some money, so make sure to sign up either individually or with a team of 3 ASAP!!
