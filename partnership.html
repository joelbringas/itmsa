Partnership

Since its inception, ITMSA has made it our mission to seek out value-added partnerships that enrich the BTM experience. Throughout the year, we provide various opportunities and events which we can help you reach your brand's strategic goals. Additionally, you will be supporting the TRSM Student Body and connecting with young eager professionals. We have several partnership options and are more than willing to offer customized plans.
