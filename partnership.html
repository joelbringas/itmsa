A Non-Profit, Student-Run Association

Our group of talented student volunteers work hard to host events geared towards the betterment of the student body, enhancing their academic, social, and professional aspects.

Throughout the year, we provide various opportunities and events which we can help you reach your brand's strategic goals. Additionally, you will be supporting the TRSM Student Body and connecting with young eager professionals.

Our mission is to establish partnerships with various organizations that provide mutual value and benefits. We have several partnership options and are more than willing to offer customized plans.

Social Tentative

Alumni Appreciation

  • In collaboration with CIO Association of Canada
  • January 31, 2018 — 6-9 PM
  • Aperture Room — 340 Yonge Street, Toronto
  • Business Formal

This a social event or faculty and alumni to reconnect. It is also a way for ITMSA to give back to the individuals who have participated in our recent events. Student attendance is also encouraged in order for them to gain career relevant information and opportunities over food and beverages.

By sponsoring this event, it will give you the opportunity to increase your brand awareness, receive media exposure, engage with a large audience, and have your business front and center. Your brand will be seen through various elements of the event such as: recognition at the event, and recruitment opportunities through the networking portion of the night.


Academic Tentative

Tech Consulting Night

  • In collaboration with RCA
  • March 2, 2018 — 6-9 PM
  • Sears Atrium — 245 Church Street (3rd Floor), Toronto
  • Business Casual

ITMSA and Ryerson Consulting Association (RCA) have partnered together this year to bring this amazing networking night to both the students and partnering firms. Firms will have the opportunity to educate the students on Technology Consulting and network with students looking to pursue a career in consulting. Your firm will get the chance to promote all their co-op/internships and new-grad opportunities to our student leaders.

Your brand will be seen through various elements of the event such as: branded merchandise in delegate bags, recognition at the event, and recruitment opportunities through a night of networking.


Academic

Ted Rogers IT Summit

  • March 13, 2018 — 6-9PM
  • Ritz Carlton — 181 Wellington Street West, Toronto
  • Business Formal

Ted Rogers IT Summit provides an opportunity for students from a variety of business academic backgrounds to expand their understanding of the pivotal integration of Information Technologies across all industries. The event aims to explore the applications of technology along with analyzing the current trends in the industry through the aid of panels, speakers, and competitions.

By sponsoring this event, it will give you the opportunity to increase your brand awareness, receive media exposure, engage with a large audience, and have your business front and center. Your brand will be seen through various elements of the event such as: branded merchandise in delegate bags, recognition at the event, and recruitment opportunities through the career fair portion of the night.
