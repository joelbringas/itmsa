Positions

All applicants must be majoring or minoring in BTM. Detailed information on positions and responsibilities are described on individual position pages, which can be viewed once you have selected the position from the drop-down menu.

Should you have any questions or concerns, please e-mail saljoq.khurshid@itmsa.ca.


Apply

Associate

Events
The Events Associate(s) will assist the Director of Academic and Director of Social Events with planning and executing all of the events the ITMSA has planned for the year. As an Events Associate you will be conducting class talks, assisting with the logistics of the events, and ensuring the successful execution of the event on the day of. In this role you will be providing creative input to assist the events team in achieving our strategic goals for the year.
Street Team
As a member of the Street Team, you will aid the marketing team in promoting the ITMSA and its upcoming events. Your primary means of doing so will be through class talks and presence at ITMSA pop-ups around TRSM. Ability to approach strangers and speak in front of an audience is a strong asset.
General Marketing
The role of the Marketing Associate will be to assist the Marketing Committee in increasing student engagement and brand awareness for the ITMSA. You will receive the opportunity to work closely with the Director of Social Media and Director of Graphics to provide creative input to assist the team in achieving our marketing targets for the year.
Corporate Relations
The role of the Corporate Relations Associate will be to aid and assist the Directors of Corporate Relations in developing strategies to attract and secure sponsorship opportunities. As a Corporate Relations Associate you will be responsible for conducting industry research to update our contact lists with potential sponsors/speakers, assist in building and maintaining professional relationships, and providing creative input to assist the corporate team in achieving our strategic goals for the year.
Case Competitions
The Case Competition Associate(s) will assist the Director of Case Competitions with planning and executing all of the competitions the ITMSA has planned for the year. As a Case Competitions Associate you will be conducting class talks, assisting with the logistics of the events, and ensuring the successful execution of the event on the day of. In this role you will be providing creative input to assist the Case Competitions team in achieving our strategic goals for the year.
