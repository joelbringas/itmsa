BTM Certification

  • November 1, 2017 — 12-3 PM
  • Cara Commons — TRS 1-148
  • Business Casual

Junior IT Case Competition (JICC)

  • In collaboration with WITM & Sponsored by Nando's
  • October 22, 2017 — 9 AM - 3 PM
  • Cara Commons — TRS 1-148
  • Business Formal

ITMSA and WITM are excited to announce that the annual Junior IT Case Competition (JICC) is back! This year, JICC will be held on October 22nd. Please note, the competition is exclusive to 1st and 2nd year BTM students.

Participating in this case competition will provide you with a chance to collaborate, build upon skills within a competitive learning environment, and leverage in-class material to solve business problems to a diverse set of industry professionals, all for a chance to win cash prizes! The team that comes in 1st place will get $1000, 2nd place will get $750 and the team in third place will get $450. Let's be real we are all students who could use some money, so make sure to sign up either individually or with a team of 3 ASAP!!

Welcome BTM

  • In collaboration with WITM & Connect IT Conference
  • September 20, 2017 — 5-7 PM
  • Cara Commons — TRS 1-148
  • Casual

To kick off the 2017-2018 school year, the ITMSA would like to present our first event “Welcome BTM”! Come out to “Welcome BTM” to connect with professors outside of classrooms, ask BTM related questions, and learn about the different resources accessible within TRSM to help enhance your academic and professional experience at Ryerson University.

Don’t miss this amazing opportunity to socialize, network, and build relationships with BTM student leaders, faculty, and students to learn more about their experiences in the BTM program. To learn more about what the ITMSA has to offer, be sure to come out!


Established in 2002, Ted Rogers School of Management's ITMSA has grown to become the premier course union for the largest Business Technology Management (BTM) student population in the country. Our executive team continually strives to seek what is in the best interests for our students in order to improve our program and equip students with the skills they'll need upon graduation. ITMSA hosts many events that not only help shape our students to become industry ready professionals after their academic career, but to also encourage more well rounded, social and corporate responsible individuals.
