Meet Our Team

For the 2017-2018 academic year, ITMSA will aim to provide students with new events and opportunities to get involved. Our team also exists to fulfill an open-door policy. We are always open to suggestions and ideas, so please feel free to reach out to us regarding any new initiatives that you would like to see throughout the year or even if you are simply seeking advice. In a literal sense, our office door is always open for you to drop by and to just sit down and have a casual chat. We wish everyone the best of luck in the upcoming academic year!

Established in 2002, ITMSA has grown to become the premier course union for the largest Business Technology Management (BTM) student population in the country. Our executive team continually strives to seek what is in the best interests for our students in order to improve our program and equip students with the skills they'll need upon graduation. ITMSA hosts many events that not only help shape our students to become industry ready professionals after their academic career, but to also encourage more well rounded, social and corporate responsible individuals.
