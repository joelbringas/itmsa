Welcome BTM

6 PM - 9 PM | Cara Commons | Business Casual

This is an event to kick off the new school year and welcome everyone, new and current BTM students, back to Ryerson. We will be inviting BTM professors and faculty members as a pre-emptive thank you for their support in the year and encourage students to get to know their professors outside of the classroom as well. Light food and refreshments will be provided and we will have interactive activities set up. This is an opportunity for all BTM students to socialize and network with people outside of their year and with professors in a relaxing environment. We hope the students meet new people in their program and create a stronger relationship with their professors. The event will be about two hours long.

Established in 2002, Ted Rogers School of Management's ITMSA has grown to become the premier course union for the largest Business Technology Management (BTM) student population in the country. Our executive team continually strives to seek what is in the best interests for our students in order to improve our program and equip students with the skills they'll need upon graduation. ITMSA hosts many events that not only help shape our students to become industry ready professionals after their academic career, but to also encourage more well rounded, social and corporate responsible individuals.
